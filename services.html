Wedding is one of the most beautiful and memorable phase of your life, to say in a line, it’s a one in a lifetime event. Imagine walking on the red carpet, holding your partner’s hand, wearing the dream groom/bride dress, under the shimmering & glittering lights, your family with you, blessings and prayers echoed all around with a shower of red petal flowers and happiness falling on your head.


Just like a fairy tale coming out to be true. Here, every second is as precious as your smile, and there are hundreds of candid moments and poses apart from the traditional socializing images which are priceless and beautiful.

To make this wonderful moment a part of your life forever, we perform a magnificent shoot, which will create beautiful images for you and your complete family. Starting from shooting at indoor places or outdoor exotic locations, we focus on capturing some casual, poppy and fun poses of couples to stand out and get little quirky in the approach. Further, the dramatic background, crazy and cool props, scene set-up & amazing outfits will not only bring your ‘poser’ out but also compliment our artistic approach in creating a kaleidoscopic & picturesque photo album & video album for your wedding which will be with you, throughout your lives.
